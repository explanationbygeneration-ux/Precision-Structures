Our Story

Experience.
Passion. Drive.

The first owner took a bold risk — buying a small truss company in what his wife called “the middle of nowhere” — with a young family and a dream to do things differently than the big guys.

“We wanted to give every customer the kind of personal attention that only a family business can deliver.”

Three decades later, Precision Structures is still family owned and operated. The shop has grown and we serve a wider region — but the original passion for craftsmanship and community hasn’t changed one bit.

We strive to take on as many clients as we can and are always pushing to do more — not just because it’s good business, but because helping people build their homes is genuinely what we love.
