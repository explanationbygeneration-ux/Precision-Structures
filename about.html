Our Story

Built by Family.
Driven by Craft.

Family-owned and operated since 1990, Precision Structures has been building trust — and trusses — across the Rocky Mountain region for over three decades.

In 1990, Precision Structures opened its doors in Hooper, Utah — a small family operation with big ambitions and a commitment to doing things the right way. The first owner took a bold risk — buying a small truss company in what his wife called "the middle of nowhere" — with a young family and a dream to do things differently than the big guys.

Where other manufacturers cut corners to boost margins, Precision doubled down on quality. Every truss was built with the same care you'd put into your own home — because that's exactly what this family believed in.

"We wanted to give every customer the kind of personal attention that only a family business can deliver."

Over 35 years of steady growth, that founding philosophy has never wavered. What started as a modest shop with a handful of employees has grown into one of the most respected truss manufacturers in the Rocky Mountain region — serving contractors, builders, and homeowners across multiple states.

To this day, Precision Structures remains family-owned and family-operated. There are no corporate boards, no distant shareholders — just a family that shows up every day with the same passion for craftsmanship that started it all. When you call Precision, you talk to someone who cares. When you place an order, it's handled by people who take pride in every joint, every plate, every delivery.

That's the Precision difference — and after 35 years, it's still what sets us apart.
