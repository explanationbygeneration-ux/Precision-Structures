## Base Package — Site Finalization & Launch

This is what it takes to get your site from "nearly done" to "confidently live."

### Scope of Work

- **Final content review round** — We generate page-by-page review PDFs of every page on the site. You mark them up (print and circle things, use a PDF editor, or just email us notes — whatever's easiest). We implement your changes.
- **Contact form backend** — Wire up the contact form to actually deliver submissions to your inbox via Azure Functions and email delivery. Right now it's front-end only.
- **SEO technical setup** — Structured data markup so Google understands your business, canonical tags, updated sitemap, and optimized meta titles and descriptions for every page.
- **Security hardening** — Content Security Policy headers, form spam protection (honeypot + rate limiting), and input sanitization.
- **Accessibility fixes** — Skip-to-content navigation link, color contrast verification against WCAG standards, and alt text audit on all images.
- **Content verification** — Copyright year update, phone numbers, email addresses, service area, and hours confirmed accurate across all pages.
- **Cross-browser and mobile testing** — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. iPhone, Android, tablet, desktop.
- **Staging review with you** — We walk through the staging site together before anything goes to production.
- **Production deployment and go-live** — We handle the merge, monitor the deployment, and verify everything works.
- **Post-launch smoke testing** — Forms tested, links checked, load times verified, SSL confirmed, analytics firing.

### Deliverables

- Working production website at your domain
- Page review PDFs for all 6 pages
- Test results documentation
- Completed go-live checklist

- **Grows with you** — Start with the base package. Add the chatbot when you're ready. Layer in SEO when you want to invest in growth. There's no lock-in and no artificial urgency.
