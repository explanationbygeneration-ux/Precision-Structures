Roof Truss Systems

Our roof trusses are manufactured at our Hooper, Utah facility using premium grade lumber and industry-leading connector plates. Every truss is engineered specifically for your project, accounting for regional snow loads, wind ratings, and all applicable building codes. From simple gable roofs to complex multi-level systems, we deliver precision-crafted trusses that arrive on-site ready for rapid installation.

  • Common, hip, valley, and specialty configurations
  • Engineered for local snow loads and wind ratings
  • Spans up to 80+ feet available
  • 2/12 to 12/12 pitch range
  • Manufactured with premium lumber and connector plates

Floor Truss Systems

Our open-web floor trusses are engineered for exceptional performance. The open-web design creates natural chases for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing runs — eliminating the need for drilling or notching and dramatically reducing on-site labor. With consistent depth across every span, you get perfectly level floors and predictable framing every time.

  • Open-web design for easy mechanical/electrical/plumbing routing
  • Long spans with minimal deflection
  • Consistent depth for level floors
  • Reduced labor costs vs. traditional framing
  • Custom depths and configurations available

Custom Design & Engineering

When standard configurations won't cut it, our in-house engineering team steps in. We specialize in the complex geometries that define striking architecture — steep pitches, multi-level roof planes, cathedral vaults, and unique structural shapes. Every custom project receives full engineering analysis and sealed drawings, giving you confidence that the design meets all code requirements and structural demands.

  • Complex hip and valley systems
  • Cathedral and vaulted ceilings
  • Multi-level and stepped roof designs
  • Energy heel and raised heel configurations
  • Full engineering sealed drawings
